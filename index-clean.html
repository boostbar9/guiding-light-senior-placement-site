Our Story

Born From Our Own Family's Journey

When our own grandfather needed help, we discovered how hard it was to find the right home. The system felt fragmented — no one seemed to look at his whole story, his medical needs, and what truly made him feel at home.

Our family was able to navigate the gaps because of two decades of senior care experience between us. Other families don't have that. We created Guiding Light Senior Placement so they don't have to go it alone.

"We walk beside families to help them find a home that fits the person — not just the diagnosis."

What We Do

Personalized Placement Services

Discharge support and placement guidance into the right level of care.

Independent Living

Active, social communities for seniors who want freedom with helpful amenities nearby.

Assisted Living

Daily-living help, medication management, and engaging programs in a home-like setting.

Memory Care

Specialized, secure environments designed for those living with Alzheimer's or dementia.

Board & Care Homes
